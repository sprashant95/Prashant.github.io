Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What undergraduate programs are offered at Parul University?

Parul University offers a wide range of undergraduate programs including B.Tech, BBA, B.Com, B.Sc, B.Pharm, B.Arch, and many more. Please visit our Programs Page for a detailed list of available programs.

What is the eligibility criteria for UG programs at Parul University?

The eligibility criteria vary depending on the program. Generally, candidates must have completed their 10+2 education from a recognized board with the required percentage of marks. Specific programs may have additional requirements such as entrance exams.

How can I apply for an undergraduate program at Parul University?

You can apply online through the Parul University admissions portal. The process involves filling out the application form, uploading required documents, and paying the application fee. For detailed instructions, please visit our Admissions Page.
